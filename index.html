SoundScape

Welcome to SoundScape!

Step into your carnival where you can:

  • Create your own music with hand gestures
  • Catch words with face movements
  • Control everything with voice commands

Available Voice Commands:

  • "Hand mode" - Select hand mode
  • "Face mode" - Select face mode
  • "Start game" - Start the selected mode
  • "Go back" - Return to main menu
  • "Sound on/off" - Toggle sound
  • "Volume up/down" - Adjust volume
Enable Voice Control

How to Play Word Catching Game

  • Use your head movements to control the green dot
  • Catch falling musical words with the green dot
  • Each word caught gives you 10 points
  • You have 60 seconds to catch as many words as possible

Voice commands available during game:

  • "Start game" - Begin playing
  • "Go back" - Return to menu
  • "Sound on/off" - Toggle sound

How to Play Hand Mode

  • Create music using simple hand gestures
  • Move your hand within the camera frame
  • Different gestures (i.e like, dislike, victory, point, open palm, fist etc.) trigger different sounds
  • Keep your hand visible to the camera

Voice commands available during game:

  • "Start game" - Begin playing
  • "Go back" - Return to menu
  • "Sound on/off" - Toggle sound
  • "Volume up/down" - Adjust volume
